About Us

We're the team behind RelayRobin

We're passionate about making it easy for people to connect, and for us, it all starts with the word "Hello."

As a division of Myplanet — a leading web application development company with offices in Toronto, Vancouver, and Ukraine — we know that in-person interactions aren't always possible. Which is why we're making software tools to bring people together, even when they're half a world apart.

We blend Agile and Lean approaches to UX, user-focused design and development, product management, and customer success into a package that is truly unique. We believe in continuous improvements to our products, our services and ourselves, which means we are always looking for feedback in how we can do better. Drop us a line to let us know how we're doing anytime — or just to say "Hello".
